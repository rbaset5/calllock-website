Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a setup fee? expand_more

No, CallLock is completely free to set up. You can start your 14-day free trial immediately without paying a cent upfront.

Can I cancel anytime? expand_more

Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time from your dashboard with one click.

Can I keep my current phone number? expand_more

Yes! We can forward your existing number to CallLock, or port it over entirely. You can also get a new local number for free with any plan.

What happens if I go over my plan limits? expand_more

We'll never cut off your calls. If you exceed your plan's included minutes, we'll simply bill you a small per-minute fee for the overage, or you can upgrade to the next tier seamlessly.
